# Form 1 Junior Science - 30 Web Applications Plan

## Theme

**Bright, Professional, Interactive, Band 1A Standard.**
Stack: HTML5, Tailwind CSS, React (CDN), Canvas/Three.js.

## Unit 1: Introduction to Science

1.  **Lab Safety Hunt** (`01_Lab_Safety`) - Interactive lab scene to identify hazards. (Ref: `Harzard Warning Label`)
2.  **Apparatus Master** (`02_Apparatus`) - Drag and drop to match apparatus names and functions.
3.  **Bunsen Burner Simulator** (`03_Bunsen_Burner`) - Virtual lighting procedure and flame types.
4.  **Measurement Lab** (`04_Measurement`) - Virtual vernier calipers, micrometer, and cylinder reading.

## Unit 2: Water

5.  **The Water Cycle** (`05_Water_Cycle`) - Interactive animation of evaporation, condensation, etc. (Ref: `Water Cycle`)
6.  **States of Matter** (`06_States_Matter`) - Particle simulation of Solid, Liquid, Gas.
7.  **Filtration & Evaporation** (`07_Filtration`) - Virtual experiment setup.
8.  **Distillation Tower** (`08_Distillation`) - Simulation of simple and fractional distillation.
9.  **Solubility & Curves** (`09_Solubility`) - Interactive graph plotter for solubility.
10. **Water Purification City** (`10_Water_Purification`) - Gamified management of water treatment.

## Unit 3: Looking at Living Things

11. **Living vs Non-Living** (`11_Living_NonLiving`) - Sorting game based on life characteristics.
12. **The Dichotomous Key** (`12_Classification_Key`) - Interactive tool to identify unknown species. (Ref: `Classification of Plant`)
13. **Vertebrates Gallery** (`13_Vertebrates`) - 3D/Image carousel of vertebrate classes.
14. **Invertebrates Inspector** (`14_Invertebrates`) - Deep dive into arthropods and others.
15. **Plant Kingdom** (`15_Plant_Kingdom`) - Flowering vs Non-flowering interactive guide.
16. **BioHK: Biodiversity** (`16_Biodiversity`) - _Existing App_. Hong Kong species focus. (Ref: `Biodiversity`)

## Unit 4: Cells & Reproduction

17. **Microscope Simulator** (`17_Microscope`) - Virtual focusing and magnification calculation. (Ref: `Microscope`)
18. **Animal Cell Builder** (`18_Animal_Cell`) - Drag and drop organelles to build a cell. (Ref: `Cell`)
19. **Plant Cell Builder** (`19_Plant_Cell`) - Compare with animal cells (cell wall, chloroplasts).
20. **Cell Division Intro** (`20_Cell_Division`) - Simple visualizer of cell splitting.

## Unit 5: Energy

21. **Energy Forms & Conversion** (`21_Energy_Forms`) - Interactive energy transfer chains.
22. **Conduction Race** (`22_Conduction`) - Particle simulation of heat transfer in rods. (Ref: `Conduction`)
23. **Convection Currents** (`23_Convection`) - Simulation of currents in water/air. (Ref: `Convection`)
24. **Radiation Laboratory** (`24_Radiation`) - Absorbers and emitters experiment (colors). (Ref: `Radiation`)
25. **Sankey Diagram Maker** (`25_Sankey_Diagram`) - Tool to generate efficiency diagrams. (Ref: `Sankey diagram`)
26. **Insulation Challenge** (`26_Insulation_Challenge`) - Design a cup to keep coffee hot.

## Unit 6: Matter as Particles

27. **Particle Theory Explorer** (`27_Particle_Theory`) - Visualizing Brownian motion and expansion. (Ref: `Particle Theory`)
28. **Density Tower** (`28_Density_Tower`) - Virtual density column with various objects. (Ref: `Castesian Diver`)
29. **Gas Pressure Simulator** (`29_Gas_Pressure`) - Volume vs Pressure vs Temp (Intro to laws).
30. **Thermal Expansion** (`30_Thermal_Expansion`) - Bimetallic strip and railway gaps simulation.
