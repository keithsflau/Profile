# Form 2 Junior Science - 30 Web Applications Plan

## Theme

**Bright, Professional, Interactive, Band 1A Standard.**
Stack: HTML5, Tailwind CSS, React (CDN), Canvas/Three.js.

## Unit 7: Living Things and Air

1.  **Photosynthesis Lab** (`01_Photosynthesis`) - Virtual bubble count experiment with light intensity.
2.  **Respiration Runner** (`02_Respiration`) - Compare aerobic vs anaerobic respiration.
3.  **Alveolar Gas Exchange** (`03_Gas_Exchange`) - Interactive diffusion model. (Ref: `Alveolar Gas Exchange Simulation`)
4.  **Air Composition** (`04_Air_Composition`) - Pie chart and gas properties interaction.
5.  **Lung Health & Smoking** (`05_Lung_Health`) - Visualizing tar accumulation and capacity.
6.  **BioCycle Explorer** (`06_BioCycle`) - Carbon and Nitrogen cycles. (Ref: `BioCycle`)

## Unit 8: Making Use of Electricity

7.  **Circuit Constructor** (`07_Circuit_Constructor`) - Drag and drop components to build series/parallel circuits.
8.  **Ohm's Law Lab** (`08_Ohms_Law`) - V=IR interactive graph and circuit. (Ref: `Ohm-s-Law`)
9.  **Electrical Safety House** (`09_Safety_House`) - Identify risks (overloading, damp).
10. **Electromagnet Crane** (`10_Electromagnet`) - Variable turns and current game.
11. **Domestic Electricity** (`11_Domestic_Bill`) - Calculate kWh and cost of appliances.

## Unit 9: Common Acids and Alkalis

12. **The pH Scale** (`12_pH_Scale`) - Interactive slider matching specific substances to pH/Colors.
13. **Neutralization Titration** (`13_Titration_Sim`) - Virtual burette and indicator color change.
14. **Indicators Laboratory** (`14_Indicators`) - Test Litmus, Methyl Orange, Phenolphthalein.
15. **Acid Rain City** (`15_Acid_Rain`) - Simulation of corrosion on buildings and statues.
16. **Reactivity Series** (`16_Reactivity`) - Adding metals to acid (pop test animation).
17. **Atomic Structure Pro** (`17_Atomic_Structure`) - Build atoms (p, n, e). (Ref: `Atomic structures`)

## Unit 10: Sensing the Environment

18. **Eye Anatomy Viewer** (`18_Eye_Anatomy`) - Interactive cross-section and function tool.
19. **Light Ray Optics** (`19_Optics_Lab`) - Convex/Concave lens simulation (correction of sight).
20. **Ear & Sound Waves** (`20_Ear_Sound`) - Visualizing pitch and frequency interactions.
21. **Skin Sensitivity** (`21_Skin_Touch`) - Mapping receptors in the dermis.
22. **Reaction Time Tester** (`22_Reaction_Time`) - Stop the light game (Nervous system speed).

## Unit 11: A Healthy Body

23. **Balanced Diet Planner** (`23_Diet_Planner`) - Drag and drop meal creation.
24. **Digestion Factory** (`24_Digestion`) - Animation of food passage and enzyme action.
25. **Heart Pump Sim** (`25_Heart_Pump`) - Double circulation visualization.
26. **Blood Component Scope** (`26_Blood_Scope`) - Identify Red/White cells and platelets.

## Unit 12: Force and Motion

27. **Friction Slider** (`27_Friction_Lab`) - Changing surfaces and measuring force.
28. **Gravity Drop** (`28_Gravity_Drop`) - Compare falling objects on Earth/Moon/Mars.
29. **Speed-Time Grapher** (`29_Motion_Graphs`) - Match the story to the graph.
30. **Newton's Forces** (`30_Newtons_Forces`) - Free body diagram editor.
